Jujitsu

One of my favourite hobbies is jujitsu. Jujitsu is a close combat form of martial arts where two people are trying to submit one another by choking the other out, or to get them to "tap".

Sparing

Along side jujitsu, another hobby I enjoy is sparing. Sparing is where two individuals "fight" at 80% or less, using the skills they have and trying new skills, punching and kicking eachother while wearing gear. The whole purpose of sparing is to help one another grow with fighting by being in a "Fight".
